About Our Chillies

Premium quality chillies grown with care in the fertile lands of Tamil Nadu

Freshly Harvested Premium Quality

We are a family-owned farm located in Puthupatti (near Innungur), in the Trichy district of Tamil Nadu. Our red chillies are grown with care and harvested at the peak of freshness to ensure the best quality and flavor.

Our chillies are known for their vibrant color and rich taste, making them perfect for all your culinary needs. We take pride in offering a product that is natural, healthy, and free from harmful chemicals.

With a commitment to sustainable farming practices, we ensure that every chilli you get is not only delicious but also environmentally friendly.
